# Modulus AppointFlow — 3-Minute Demo Script

**Purpose**: a short (≤ 3 min) Loom / screen-recording walkthrough to send to
Lemon Squeezy reviewers, payment partners, beta testers or the press.

**Target length**: 2:30 – 3:00
**Voice**: calm, factual, English — imagine a busy shop owner watching on 1.25x.
**Camera**: screen-only, no webcam. 1080p or 1440p.
**Audio**: quiet room, mic ~15 cm from mouth. Avoid keyboard sound on mic.

---

## Before you hit record (5-min prep)

- [ ] Close every chat / notification-capable app.
- [ ] Browser: new incognito window, zoom 110%, bookmark bar hidden.
- [ ] Open the following tabs in order (left → right):
  1. `https://modulusaas.com/products/appointflow`
  2. A second tab with a WhatsApp Web or a phone mirror showing a test
     conversation named **"Bella Hair Salon"** (or any fake salon name) — two
     pre-typed draft messages ready.
  3. `https://modulusaas.com/en/refund` (will be used briefly at the end).
- [ ] Put your laptop on charger; disable Wi-Fi sleep.
- [ ] Have a glass of water next to you.

---

## Segment 1 · The hook (0:00 – 0:20)

Cursor hovering over the AppointFlow hero image.

> "Hi, this is Modulus AppointFlow — a fully autonomous appointment agent that
> lives inside WhatsApp and Google Calendar.
>
> In the next three minutes I'll show you exactly what a small business gets
> when they sign up for 29 dollars a month."

*(Scroll slowly down the hero section so viewers see the chat demo bubble.)*

---

## Segment 2 · The problem (0:20 – 0:40)

Continue scrolling to the "Problem" section.

> "The customers we serve — barbers, beauty salons, dental clinics, pet
> groomers, personal trainers — lose 20 to 40 percent of revenue to missed
> calls, late-night messages and no-shows.
>
> AppointFlow replies in nine languages, 24 / 7, from a phone number that
> looks like the business's own."

---

## Segment 3 · How it works (0:40 – 1:20)

Stop on the three-step "How it works" section.

> "Step one — the business connects their Google Calendar once. Step two —
> they point their WhatsApp Business number at our webhook. Step three — an
> AI agent starts taking messages the same minute."

*(Switch to the WhatsApp tab. Show a pre-scripted conversation.)*

- **Customer** (WhatsApp voice or text): "Hi, can I book a haircut Friday?"
- **AppointFlow agent** (within ~2 s): "Of course! I can offer Friday 14:00,
  15:30 or 17:00 with Marco. Which one works?"
- **Customer**: "15:30 please."
- **AppointFlow agent**: "Booked. You'll get a reminder 24 hours and 2 hours
  before. Reply CANCEL any time."

> "Everything you just saw is one Edge Function call — no human involved. The
> event lands on our tenant's Google Calendar in real time."

---

## Segment 4 · The autonomous agents (1:20 – 1:50)

Back to the product page, scroll to the "11 agents" section.

> "What makes AppointFlow different from a chatbot is that it's eleven small
> autonomous agents working together: qualification, conversion, onboarding,
> messaging, scheduling, reminders, retention, billing, pricing, and
> compliance — all orchestrated by a Supabase Edge Function we call the
> orchestrator.
>
> They not only answer incoming messages — they also reach out to lapsed
> clients, send reminders, handle no-show recovery, and retry failed payments.
> The owner wakes up to more bookings, not more work."

---

## Segment 5 · Pricing & trust (1:50 – 2:30)

Scroll to the pricing table.

> "Three tiers — Starter at 29, Pro at 49, Business at 99 dollars a month,
> all with a 7-day free trial. Billing is handled globally by Lemon Squeezy
> as Merchant of Record, so we stay focused on the product while they manage
> tax compliance worldwide."

*(Hover over the "Yearly" toggle to show the –17 % badge.)*

> "Yearly saves 17 percent."

*(Switch briefly to the `/en/refund` tab and show that legal pages exist.)*

> "Refunds, cancellation, privacy and terms are all published in English and
> Turkish. Cancel any time from the dashboard — no phone-call required."

---

## Segment 6 · Close (2:30 – 3:00)

Back to the landing page, hover over the main CTA button.

> "That's Modulus AppointFlow — autonomous appointments on WhatsApp,
> powered by Google Calendar and available worldwide from today.
>
> If you'd like a sandbox to poke around, reply to this email and I'll send
> you a test tenant within the hour.
>
> Thanks for watching!"

Stop recording. Let the last frame of the landing page sit for 0.5 s.
